About us

Our visions

  • G- Galvanising Unity among Diversity in Health
  • W- working for the progress of Healthcare in the UK and Nigeria
  • G- Growing always through continuous Self-Empowerment

Our Values

  • Unity in Love
  • Mutual respect
  • Innovation
  • Altruism

How we met

  • A certain doctor met a nurse in the library and explained the need to form a coomunity among Nigerian nurses, doctors and other health nigerian care professionals in the UK

Referrals

  • You can make Referrals or experess your wish to join our community by filling the referrals or sign up button

Why the name

  • Green stands for wealth, White Signifies Peace, Love and Unity
  • Heroes are people who are admired by their courage, outstanding achievements or noble qualities
  • Healthcare - we are professionals in the healthcare sector and are based in the uk

Leadership

  • The group is equally coordinated by health care professionals (nurses, doctors, care workers , radiographesr, phlebotomists e.tc.) from various field based on their expertise and creativity
